About Us

Auriel is a newly founded art and design brand that aims to bring creativity and beauty to everyday life. Established in 2021, Auriel is committed to showcasing the work of talented artists and designers from Auriel, while providing customers with high-quality products that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. From home decor to prints, Auriel's diverse range of products is designed to inspire and uplift, adding a touch of artistry and elegance to any space or outfit. With a passion for creativity and a dedication to excellence, Auriel is quickly becoming a go-to destination for those who appreciate the beauty and power of art and design.
